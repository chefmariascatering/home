ABOUT CHEF MARIA'S

Chef Maria’s, our name was a collaboration by four long-time friends and former colleagues. We started our journey on 8/8/2020, in the middle of a global pandemic. We were driven by the dream to provide the best quality and service through our food and styling. We have been in the event and food & catering industry for a total of 10 years before Chef Maria’s Catering Services. With extensive expertise and experience, we decided to create our own lasting legacy.
